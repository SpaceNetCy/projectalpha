How we work.

Renovation Group prides itself on the quality of our service.

1

Step One: Visit Our Showroom

We recommend that our relationship begin with your visit to our showroom. Here you will be able to view the various kitchen models we have on display from ten quality manufacturers with cabinets at every price point and design. There are product choices to meet every customer’s style and taste.
When you visit us we’ll discuss:

General kitchen design ideas you may be considering. 
What cabinet line is best for you. 
A reasonable budget expectation for your project. 
Other options you may be contemplating.

2

Step Two: A Home Visit

After your showroom visit, we will come to your home for a measure up of the room(s), and continue our design discussion. At the conclusion of this meeting, we will review:

You have identified a cabinet product line that satisfies your quality and taste preferences.
Based on our discussions, together we have developed a design concept that reflects your vision of your new kitchen.
At our next meeting we will submit a design package that includes your choices and an estimated project cost that will be within your budget requirements.

3

Step Three: The Retainer Meeting

At this meeting, we will:

Present a proposed contract with complete kitchen drawings including floor plans, perspectives and elevations.
Plumbing, electrical and flooring detail will also be reviewed if included in the project.
Upon meeting our commitment to you, we will ask for a non-refundable retainer equal to 10% of the proposed contract, subject to a minimum. The retainer is an initial deposit – not a design fee – and the entire retainer value is credited toward your total purchase.
